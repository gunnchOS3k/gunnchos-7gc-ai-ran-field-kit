# Thesis — 30 Seconds

**Title:** Resilience-Aware, Human-Centric AI-RAN Orchestration for Service Continuity Across Terrestrial and Non-Terrestrial Networks

---

## Script (~75 words)

I study how privacy-preserving device context and digital-twin state can inform AI-RAN orchestration to keep services running when terrestrial links degrade and NTN or local-edge fallbacks are needed. Three questions cover trustworthy measurement, twin-informed policies versus strong baselines, and when multi-access fallback helps or fails. The pipeline is built and preregistered; authentic pilot collection is in progress at zero of fifty-four eligible sessions—evaluation design is ready but not yet run on complete physical data.
