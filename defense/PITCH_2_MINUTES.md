## Script

**[0:00–0:20] Hook**

Affordable edge users do not experience 6G as peak throughput—they experience interruptions. When terrestrial service degrades, fallback paths through local edge or non-terrestrial links can help or hurt, especially if orchestration ignores workload intent and privacy constraints. My dissertation asks whether twin-informed, privacy-preserving AI-RAN orchestration can improve service continuity and worst-user quality of experience compared to static and network-only policies.

**[0:20–0:50] Three questions**

Three locked research questions structure the work. First: how to build reproducible digital-twin state from Edge-IO measurements without direct identifiers. Second: whether twin-informed orchestration beats preregistered baselines on continuity, fairness, and efficiency. Third: under which outage, mobility, and energy conditions multi-access and NTN fallback actually work—and where they fail under measurement uncertainty.

**[0:50–1:20] Method**

Methodologically, I integrate five research repositories behind canonical JSON contracts: measurement, twin state, AI-RAN policies, and NTN resilience simulation, with the field-kit as evidence authority. Gate 2 confirms the full path executes with schema validation. A fifty-four-session local pilot is preregistered; eligible collection is at zero of fifty-four while zones and dates await approval. Gate 4 evaluation is preregistered with recovery time as the primary outcome—but scientific evaluation on complete physical data has not yet been executed.

**[1:20–1:50] Contributions**

Expected contributions are reproducible measurement and twin contracts, preregistered orchestration evaluation with explicit failure boundaries, and honest evidence taxonomy separating system completion from scientific pass. Three papers map to the three questions. I do not claim finished commercial products, deployment-scale 6G, or global generalization from a single pilot site.

**[1:50–2:00] Close**

I am seeking supervision aligned with experimental wireless and edge-intelligent RAN research—and I report gate status exactly as the evidence shows today.
