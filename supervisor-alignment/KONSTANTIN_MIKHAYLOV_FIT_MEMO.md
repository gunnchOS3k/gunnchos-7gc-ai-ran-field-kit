## 1. Faculty research summary (public sources)

Associate Professor Mikhaylov (Title of Docent) focuses on **convergent wireless for IoT**, machine-type connectivity, LPWAN, energy-efficient protocols, and **integration of MTC with satellite / non-terrestrial infrastructure toward 6G**. Public project pages describe RedCap-over-NTN research, direct-to-satellite IoT link feasibility, and workshops on integrating MTC and satellites. He holds Academy of Finland PostDoc experience (2021–2024) and extensive IoT device/system design background.
