## 1. Faculty research summary (public sources)

Professor Pouttu directs 6G Flagship and leads experimental network research including the University’s 5G/6G test network (5GTN/6GTN+). Public profiles emphasize digital transmission, dependable wireless for verticals (energy, health, industry, automotive), cognitive networks, and **AI-driven control and optimization of complex radio networks** using experimental infrastructure. He previously served as CWC Director (2006–2012) and holds extensive project leadership experience across European satellite/terrestrial initiatives.
