## 1. Faculty research summary (public sources)

Professor Bennis leads the ICON group at CWC. Public profiles highlight **radio resource management, game theory, and distributed AI in 5G/6G networks**, with extensive work on **edge intelligence, federated learning, communication-efficient ML, and wireless network intelligence at the edge**. He is an IEEE Fellow, Clarivate Highly Cited Researcher (multiple years), and active in 6G Flagship. Research emphasizes co-design of wireless systems and machine learning under latency, reliability, privacy, and resource constraints.
