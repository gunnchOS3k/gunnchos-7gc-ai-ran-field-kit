# Privacy and Ethics (Umbrella)

Portfolio-wide privacy rules for **phone-first field console** and **receive-only SDR observation** research.

## Principles

1. **No private third-party traffic payload collection**
2. **No packet payload inspection**
3. **Opt-in only** with documented consent version
4. **No minors** without guardian/program approval
5. **Aggregated reporting** for public results
6. **Retention and deletion** per campaign policy
7. **Public Wi-Fi boundaries** — measure participant device experience only
8. **Receive-only RF** where SDR is discussed — no unauthorized transmission
